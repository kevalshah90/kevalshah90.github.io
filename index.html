About Me

Here's a short bio written in third-person

Keval Shah is a Data Scientist with experience working in Advertising and Policy Research. Currently, Keval is working on a Marketplace Startup in Commercial Real Estate space. Keval was born in Mumbai, India and emigrated to the United States when he was 19 years old. He is educated, with a BS in Computer Science from Mumbai University and an MS in Analytics from the University of Chicago. He's lived in 3 different cities including his hometown Mumbai, Chicago and now San Francisco. He enjoys traveling, tennis and staying active by practicing yoga, riding bike and hiking.

My Story

My professional career began when I landed my first job in marketing working as a Developer building mobile products. Through my work, I worked closely with Analytics for capturing consumer information. It got me curious about the field and I started researching about data science. I made the decision to pursue graduate studies in Analytics at University of Chicago and was one of the first to enroll into the program.

During graduate school, I had the opportunity to travel to Japan on a NSF research fellowship to work at AIST. My work was focused on mining social media text, temporal and location data to build a visual analytics tool. The goal was to understand geo-temporal patterns in topics of interests.

For the last year of graduate school, I was holding down a corporate job in Advertising working full time and taking classes in the evening. On paper, my life was fairly successful. However, I didn't particularly feel accomplished and didn't quite understand what was missing. Upon graduating, I decided to take some time off and travel across Europe to France, Austria and Switzerland. I spent the next year working in different industries, pursuing my interest in Econometrics and taking time to travel more. I worked in Finance, Market Research and Public Policy.

My interests are Policy Research, Econometric theory and Machine Learning, and Experimental Methods.

I recently moved out to the San Francisco Bay Area.
